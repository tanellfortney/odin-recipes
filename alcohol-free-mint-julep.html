Alcohol-Free Mint Julep

Sip of the Summer!

Imagine a crystal-clear tumbler, its sides frosted with condensation. Within, a symphony of flavors awaits—a harmonious blend of cooling mint, zesty lemon, and sparkling effervescence. This is no ordinary drink; it’s an elixir of summer sophistication.

  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint leaves
  • 2 cups crushed ice
  • 1/2 cup prepared lemonade
  • fresh mint sprigs, for garnish

Steps

  1. Combine water, sugar, and 1 tablespoon of chopped mint in a small saucepan; cook and stir until mixture has boiled and sugar has dissolved. Remove from heat and set aside to cool for about an hour; strain out mint leaves.
  2. Fill 2 cups or frozen goblets with crushed ice. Pour 1/2 of the lemonade into each glass and top with a splash of cooled sugar syrup. Garnish each with a mint sprig and a straw. Serve on a silver platter.
